# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 27 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the In Salah mobile application ("the App") handles user data. The App is a free Islamic prayer-time and missed-prayer tracking utility.

## 1. Data we collect

The App may collect or process the following data, only when needed for a feature you actively use:

| Data | Purpose | Stored where |
|---|---|---|
| Approximate or precise location | Calculating prayer times for your geographic area | On your device only — not sent to our servers |
| Email address | Account creation and sign-in | Encrypted at rest in our backend (InsForge) |
| Display name (optional) | Showing your profile inside the App | Backend, linked to your account |
| Profile picture (optional) | Showing your profile inside the App | Backend, linked to your account |
| Prayer-tracking history | Showing missed prayer statistics, syncing across devices | Local device + backend, linked to your account |
| Prayer settings (calculation method, madhab, iqama offsets, reminders, language, interface scale) | Preserving your preferences and scheduling local reminders | Local device + backend, linked to your account |

We do **not** collect:

- Contacts, calendar, photos beyond an optional avatar you choose to upload
- Microphone, camera, SMS, call logs
- Advertising identifiers
- Browsing or app-usage data outside the App

## 2. How we use your data

- **Location** is used solely on-device to compute prayer times via the standard Islamic calculation methods (MWL, ISNA, Egyptian, Umm al-Qura, Karachi). Coordinates are not transmitted to us.
- **Account data** (email, optional name, optional avatar) is used for authentication, profile display, and so your prayer history can sync across your devices.
- **Prayer history and settings** are stored so you can review your statistics and resume on a new device after sign-in.

## 3. Data sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing.

We use the following sub-processors strictly to operate the service:

- **InsForge** — backend, authentication, and database storage
- **Google Sign-In** — optional OAuth provider, only if you choose to sign in with Google

We may disclose data only when required by law (court order, lawful government request).

## 4. Data retention

- **Local data** (settings, prayer history cached on the device) is retained until you uninstall the App or clear app data.
- **Account data** in our backend is retained while your account is active. Once you request deletion, all account-linked data is permanently removed within 30 days.

## 5. Your rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the data linked to your account — visible inside the App in your profile and prayer history.
- **Correct** profile data — edit it from within the App settings.
- **Delete** your account and all associated data — see the [Account Deletion guide]({{ '/account-deletion/' | relative_url }}).
- **Withdraw permission** at any time via the system permission settings (Location, Notifications) on your device. The App will continue to work but features dependent on those permissions will be limited.

## 6. Children

The App is suitable for all ages but is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can delete it.

## 7. Security

Account data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in encrypted form by our backend provider. No system is perfectly secure, but we follow standard industry practices to minimize risk.

## 8. Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, notify users in the App.
