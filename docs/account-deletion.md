# How to delete your In Salah account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data either from inside the App or by contacting support.

## Option 1 — Delete from inside the App (recommended)

1. Open **In Salah** on your device.
2. Sign in if you are not already signed in.
3. Open the **Profile** tab from the bottom navigation.
4. Tap **Settings** (gear icon).
5. Scroll to the **Account** section.
6. Tap **Delete account**.
7. Confirm the action when prompted.

The action is **immediate and irreversible**. You will be signed out automatically.

## Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the App, send an email to **ibrohimovtv@gmail.com** from the email address associated with your account, with the subject line:

> Delete my In Salah account

We will verify the request and complete deletion within **30 days**.

## What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

- Your email address and authentication record
- Your display name and profile picture (if uploaded)
- Your prayer-tracking history synced to our backend
- Your prayer settings synced to our backend (calculation method, madhab, iqama offsets, reminders, language, interface scale)

## What stays on your device

Data cached locally on your device — settings and prayer history saved in local storage — is **not** affected by account deletion. To remove it, uninstall the App or clear its data from your device's system settings.

## Retention exceptions

We do not retain account-linked personal data after deletion. Aggregated, anonymized analytics that cannot identify a specific user may be retained for service-improvement purposes.

## Contact

If you have questions about account deletion, contact **ibrohimovtv@gmail.com**.
